question: "If a model, that was already in a fighting rank, steps up into another fighting rank, can that model attack that turn?"
referenced_rules: |
  Stepping Forward & Closing In CRB 150
  GW FAQ

The model that steps up may only direct attacks against an enemy unit that it could have attacked prior to stepping up. Use the model's original position to determine what it can attack and how many attacks it can make.
