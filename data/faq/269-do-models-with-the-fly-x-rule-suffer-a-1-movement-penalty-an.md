question: "Do models with the fly (x) rule suffer a -1 movement penalty and have to use the lowest of 2d6 when charging while starting their movement in terrain."
referenced_rules: |
  Fly (X) CRB 169

Yes, models with fly (x) always begin their movement on the ground, and any unit that start their movement in terrain suffers its effects.
