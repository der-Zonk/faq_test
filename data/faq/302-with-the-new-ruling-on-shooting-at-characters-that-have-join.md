question: "With the new ruling on shooting at characters that have joined a unit with different troop type, how do you process that shooting?"
referenced_rules: |
  GW FAQ
---
The character is targeted instead of the unit. All Line of Sight and Range are measured towards the character. Note that the unit is not able to use evasive or similar special rules and the character cannot be protected by 'Lone character' rules.
