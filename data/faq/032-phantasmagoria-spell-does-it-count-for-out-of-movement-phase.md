question: "Phantasmagoria Spell - Does it count for out of movement phase movements? Does it trigger if you don't move?"
referenced_rules: |
  Phantasmagoria CRB 325
answer: |
  It will trigger for any movement (in or out of phase) that ends within range - but it will not trigger if you choose not to move.
