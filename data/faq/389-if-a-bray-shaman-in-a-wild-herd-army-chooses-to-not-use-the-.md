question: "If a bray-shaman in a Wild Herd army chooses to not use the +1 to cast or dispel from Dark Coven on a spell that they miscast, do friendly wizards within their command range still suffer the effect of that miscast?"

No. The miscast is shared with friendly wizards only when a bray-shaman chooses to use the +1 from Dark Coven before rolling to cast or dispel.
