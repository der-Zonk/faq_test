question: "Does a unit have to do peril tests if it overlaps with the unit it is fleeing from after pivoting center-to-center away?"
referenced_rules: |
  Fleeing through Enemy Units CRB 133
change_log: |
  06.12.24: Entry
  02.12.25: Change due to majority vote shift
answer: |
  No, the unit is not fleeing "through" the unit it overlaps after the pivot, and does not make peril tests for models moving through the unit it is fleeing away from from in this way. Note: The unit must still make peril tests if it flees through any other units it is engaged with
