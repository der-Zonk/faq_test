question: "According to GW's FAQ, the Griffon Standard allows its unit to claim a higher rank bonus than normal for its unit type. How exactly does this work?"
referenced_rules: |
  Empire
---
The unit can claim their rank bonus up to the normal maximum for their unit type, but gets +2 instead of +1 for each of these ranks. Infantry with horde have a max of +6 (normally +3), whereas cavalry have a max of +2 (normally +1).
