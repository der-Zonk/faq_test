question: "Is there a difference between rules that say you suffer a wound and ones that say you lose a wound?"

  Regeneration GW FAQ 1.5.1 3
change_log: |

Suffering a wound allows armor and ward saves if not specifically ruled otherwise. Losing a wound means the model loses the wound with only Regeneration saves allowed (if not ruled out by any special rules).
