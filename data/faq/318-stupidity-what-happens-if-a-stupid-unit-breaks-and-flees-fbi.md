question: "Stupidity: What happens if a stupid unit breaks and flees, FBIGO or gives ground?"
referenced_rules: |
  GW FAQ
  Stupidity CRB 178
---
The unit does flee, give ground etc, but cannot reform when rallying, changing face or doing similar abilities - it will just conduct flee moves and give ground. Note that this also mean that you cannot use TMP or similar spells on the unit.
