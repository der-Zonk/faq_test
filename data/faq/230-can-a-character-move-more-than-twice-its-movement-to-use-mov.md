question: "Can a character move more than twice its movement to use 'Move through the ranks' or when he joins a unit to reach the front rank?"
referenced_rules: |
  GW FAQ

Yes. Since the fighting rank is at a fixed location, he is obliged to move that distance to get to the fighting rank. The only times when a character inside of a unit is subject to this (besides normal maneuvering/moving) is Reform and Redress the ranks.
