question: "What is considered the front and rear corner of a unit in Lance Formation for the purpose of determining if it can pass through other units?"
referenced_rules: |
  Bretonnia The Lance Formation FOF 110 - 111
---
For this purpose, the front edge of the second rank and the model in the first rank cannot pass through units. The rest of the unit can wheel through other units as the models would take a direct route as described in the GW FAQ.
