question: "What happens when a unit charges a unit and has no other choice but to charge in a way that a third (opponent) unit is within 1\" of the charging unit"
referenced_rules: |
  The 1" Rule CRB 118
---
Since charge moves are conducted in the movement phase, the 1" rule comes to use and the units have to be nudged 1" away
