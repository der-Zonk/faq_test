question: "How would situations resolved where a character without the First Charge rule joins a unit that has it and vice versa?"
referenced_rules: |
  First Charge CRB 169
answer: |
  As long as one model with the rule First Charge is performing its first charge of the game within a unit, the special rule is used and counts as being used for all models that participated in that charge and have the First Charge special rule in that unit
