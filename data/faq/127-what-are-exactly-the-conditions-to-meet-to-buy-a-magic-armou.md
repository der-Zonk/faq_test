question: "What are exactly the conditions to meet to buy a magic armour on a character?"
referenced_rules: |
  Magic Armour CRB 340
---
To buy a magical shield, your character must be (or has the option to be) equipped with a shield. To buy a magical suit of armour, your character must be (or has the option to be) equipped with any suit of armour (light/heavy/plate), natural armour (such as calloused hide) excluded. To buy any other magical armour that is not an suit of armour or a shield, your character must be (or has the option to be) equipped with either a shield or any armour, natural armour excluded.I.E. a skink priest cannot be given any kind of magical armour even if it has calloused hide
