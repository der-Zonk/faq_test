question: "Since AP modifies the Dice Roll and not the Armour Save, does the Armour of Meteoric Iron work against attacks with Armour Piercing (X)?"

Yes, the intent of the Armour of Meteoric Iron is clearly to prevent Armour Piercing to apply. Note that the Armour of Meteoric Iron is a heavy Armour as noted in the italics and thus a suit of armour.
