question: "Regarding the Challenges & Mounts Change - how do you determine which attacks are lost?"
referenced_rules: |
  GW FAQ
answer: |
  If one participant of the challenge is slain in one initiative step, all attacks with a lower initiative in this combat are lost and will not be conducted. You can only score overkill from attacks of the same initiative of the step in which the participant is slain.
