category: "Basic Rules"
question: "What happens when a unit that gives ground cannot move more than 1\" and will be within 1\" of a unit it is in combat with, be it because of terrain or other units?"
referenced_rules: |
  Give Ground CRB 134
---
Since giving ground is not possible, the units remain locked in combat, but all units move as far as possible and remain locked at the new position (see GW FAQ).
