question: "Do Ethereal Units have to make Peril tests when fleeing through another unit?"
referenced_rules: |
  Ethereal CRB 167
---
Yes, while they cannot be wounded by magical attacks, Peril Tests cause a unit to lose a wound, not to suffer a wound, so units with the Ethereal special rule have to do peril tests just as other units do.
