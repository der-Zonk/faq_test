question: "If you lost frenzy, can you regain it by another source?"
referenced_rules: |
  Frenzy CRB 170
  GW FAQ
---
Yes you can. You can even re-gain frenzy by the same source, like Blood Rage. Note that if a BSB with a Banner or a character with an source that grants Frenzy (and does not state it can be regained or never lost) loses frenzy and joins a new unit, that unit won't be frenzied.
