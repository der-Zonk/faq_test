question: "If a unit has to test for Restrain or Impetuous and wants to use the Inspiring Presence of a General,which is not inside the unit but has a modified leadership from warband, can the unit use the warband modifier on that generals modified leadership for this test?"
referenced_rules: |
  Warband CRB 180
  GW FAQ
answer: |
  Yes. The unit uses the modified leadership of the general via Inspiring Presence and does not use the Warband Modifier - the General uses it. The GW FAQ notes that only "a Warband" and thus the unit itself cannot use it.
