question: "How many wounds are counted for Combat Result/Overkill when models are killed with Killing Blow"
referenced_rules: |
  Multiple Wound Models CRB 102Killing Blow CRB 172Killing Blow GW FAQ 1.5.1 7
change_log: |

answer: |
  Each attack that inflicts Killing Blow or similar ability inflicts a number of wounds equal to the remaining wounds of the model. Excess wounds with Killing Blow count in challenges as one single wound.
