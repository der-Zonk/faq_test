question: "Does the \"Unity and Harmony\" rule affect Cathay units joined by Mercenary characters?"
referenced_rules: |
  Grand Cathay
answer: |
  No, Jade Warriors & Jade Lancers may only reroll To-Hit rolls of 1 if they are within 3 inches of a mercenary unit. Mercenary characters joined to non-mercenary units do not count.
