question: "Do modifiers to the To Hit roll apply when using the Seal of Parravon?"
referenced_rules: |
  Bretonnia
---
Yes. The Seal of Parravon refers to a roll of 3 on the To Hit table before modifiers (i.e., the result after consulting the table, not the natural dice roll). Modifiers are applied as normal.
