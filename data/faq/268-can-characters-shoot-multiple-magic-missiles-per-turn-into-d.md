question: "Can characters shoot multiple magic missiles per turn into different targets? A lone character would still be treated as a unit and therefore has to target the same unit?"
referenced_rules: |
  Characters and Shooting CRB 209
---
Yes he can, the exemption for magic missiles on 209 is to be applied - a character can divide his magic missiles into different targets, chosing targets when each spell is cast.
