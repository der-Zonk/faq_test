question: "How is the -1 to hit from Fear applied in multi-unit combats if the Leadership test is failed?"
referenced_rules: |
  Fear CRB 168
---
The -1 is applied to the unit that fails the test against all units that cause fear and have a higher US then the unit that failed the test in this combat.
