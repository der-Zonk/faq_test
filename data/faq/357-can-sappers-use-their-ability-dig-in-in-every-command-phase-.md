question: "Can Sappers use their ability \"Dig in!\" in every command phase or only in their own?"
referenced_rules: |
  Dwarf Mountain Holds

The wording of the special rule "Dig in!" has priority over the general rules for command abilities, thus the special rule can be used in every command phase.
