question: "If a cannon can see a large target over other models, can it choose a target point that it cannot see due to the presence of intervening models?"
referenced_rules: |
  Cannon Fire CRB 226
change_log: |

No, a cannon must have LoS to its target point. It could target the edge of the large target's base, but intervening models block LoS to points on the ground. If the cannon is on a hill or is a large target itself, then it can see over intervening models accordingly.
