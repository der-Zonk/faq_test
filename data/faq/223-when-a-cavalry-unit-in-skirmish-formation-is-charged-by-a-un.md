question: "When a cavalry unit in Skirmish formation is charged by a unit, if the impact does not happen in the \"visual front\" of the model, shall the impacted model pivot to present its logical \"visual front\" to the charging unit ?"
referenced_rules: |
  Skirmishers in Combat CRB 185
---
Yes, the Skirmish formation rule says the models form up "edge-to-edge and front corner to front corner, against the enemy", which cannot have other sense than "visual front" for a unit with only front arcs.
