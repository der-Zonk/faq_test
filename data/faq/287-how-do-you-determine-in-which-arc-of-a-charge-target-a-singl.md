question: "How do you determine in which arc of a charge target a single model is?"
referenced_rules: |
  GW FAQ
---
Only check the front edge of the model - if the majority of the front edge of a model is in one arc, then charge this arc. If you cannot come to an agreement with your opponent, charge the arc which offers the lower combat resolution
