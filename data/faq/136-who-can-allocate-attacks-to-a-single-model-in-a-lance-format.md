question: "Who can allocate attacks to a single model in a Lance Formation?"
referenced_rules: |
  Bretonnia The Lance Formation FOF 110 - 111
---
All models in both fighting ranks can allocate attacks to a single model in the enemies fighting rank. This also means that every model in each fighting rank can do all it's attacks, even it is not physically in base contact with either unit.
